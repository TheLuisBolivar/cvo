Staff Backend Engineer — Payments Platform
Company: Kushki (LATAM Fintech)
Mode: Remote from LATAM

About the role

- Production Kubernetes experience.
- Experience designing APIs (REST and gRPC).
- Intermediate-advanced English (engineering works in English).

Nice to have

- Experience with OpenTelemetry, Prometheus, distributed tracing.
- Open-source contributions.
- Experience with Terraform / IaC.
- Prior technical leadership (staff/principal/tech lead).
